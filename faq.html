Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of items do you make?

We make custom items including:

  • Plaques
  • Pens
  • Laser Engraving
  • Holiday Ornaments and decorations
  • Cutting Boards
What is the turn around time?

It depends on the item ordered and the quantity requested. Plaques can be made in a couple days. Larger quantity orders require more time.

Do you offer shipping?

At this time we do not offer shipping. If you live within a 20 mile radius of Brown Deer High School in Wisconsin, we can arrange for pick up or delivery.

Are ink refills available for the pens you sell?

Yes. Slimline pen refills can be purchased through Amazon.
